TEDxSRMIST

TEDxSRMIST is a grassroot-level, independent TED event. Born from the belief that ideas worth spreading exist right here, within our own community. We provide a space where students, thinkers, and innovators come together and celebrate the full spectrum of human curiosity and potential. Expect a day of powerful talks, bold ideas, and conversations that linger long after the lights go down. Because the next big idea? It might just come from somewhere within you.

MOSAIC : FRAGMENTS OF BECOMING

Mosaic, the flagship theme of TEDxSRMIST, celebrates the idea that we are shaped not by a single moment, but by countless fragments—experiences, choices, failures, and growth. Join us on April 10, 2026 at 9 am IST, as we explore the pieces that define us—where every story, encounter, and turning point comes together to form a mosaic that is imperfect, evolving, and uniquely ours. WHY ATTEND TEDxSRMIST?

It takes one match to light up a fire. It takes one idea to change everything. And it can stay with you longer than you expect. It makes you pause, rethink, and see things in a way that you never thought you would. TEDxSRMIST is a safe space for these ideas, thoughts and experiences. It is a place where perspectives can shift to something much more meaningful. Growing with such a platform means allowing yourself to listen, reflect, and evolve. And ultimately realizing that sometimes, the smallest idea can leave the biggest impact.
